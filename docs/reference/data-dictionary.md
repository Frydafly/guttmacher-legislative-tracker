# Fields in more detail...

## BillType Field Key

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  H - Standard legislation introduced in a state\'s House of
  Representatives\
  S - Standard legislation introduced in a state\'s Senate\
  A - Standard legislation introduced in state Assemblies (used in states
  like NY, CA, WI, NV that use \"Assembly\" instead of \"House\")\
  SJR - Senate Joint Resolution - Used for constitutional amendments or
  major policy statements, proposed jointly by Senate and House\
  CACR - Constitutional Amendment Concurrent Resolution - Specifically
  used to propose state constitutional amendments\
  HJR - House Joint Resolution - Similar to SJR but initiated in House;
  used for constitutional amendments or major policy statements\
  LR - Legislative Resolution - General resolution that can originate in
  either chamber\
  D - Draft Bill - Preliminary version of legislation not yet formally
  introduced\
  SR - Senate Resolution - Resolution involving only Senate, doesn\'t
  require House approval\
  HM - House Memorial - Formal statement or petition from House, often
  addressing federal government or other states\
  ACR - Assembly Concurrent Resolution - Needs both Assembly and Senate
  approval but not governor\'s signature (in states with Assembly)\
  HD - House Document - Formal document or draft bill in House\
  LSR - Legislative Service Request - Request for legislation to be
  drafted (commonly used in New Hampshire)\
  SCR - Senate Concurrent Resolution - Needs both Senate and House
  approval but not governor\'s signature
